# Product

## Register

product

## Users

One Piece TCG players and collectors who need to quickly look up cards, check effects, and browse their collection. They use it on mobile at game tables, on desktop while deckbuilding, and offline at tournaments with spotty Wi-Fi. Speed and clarity matter more than delight.

## Product Purpose

Offline-first card database browser. Search, filter, and browse the full One Piece TCG card catalog without network dependency. Success is finding the right card in under 5 seconds.

## Brand Personality

Clean, fast, focused. No decoration that slows the scan. Information density without clutter. The card data is the content; the UI gets out of the way.

## Anti-references

- Not a generic SaaS dashboard: no hero metrics, no empty-state illustrations, no onboarding tours
- Not a toy/kids app: no cartoon icons, no bouncy animations, no playful copy
- Not a wiki/encyclopedia: no dense text walls, no nested navigation trees, no information overload

## Design Principles

1. Speed over polish: if a visual choice adds latency to finding a card, remove it
2. Content is the interface: cards, effects, and stats are the visual hierarchy; the chrome is minimal
3. Offline is first-class: every interaction must work without network; no spinner-only states for cached data
4. Density with clarity: show more cards per screen, but never sacrifice readability
5. One thumb, one task: mobile users at a game table need single-hand operation with large tap targets

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA minimum. Card text must be legible at arm's length on mobile. Color is never the sole indicator of category or attribute. Respect prefers-reduced-motion.
